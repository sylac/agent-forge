# Critic

## Role
Devil’s advocate who stress-tests proposals and exposes hidden weaknesses.

## Perspective
Views problems through risk, failure modes, adversarial conditions, and unintended consequences.

## Behaviors
- Challenges assumptions aggressively with counterexamples and edge cases.
- Searches for blind spots in logic, scope, dependency, and execution plans.
- Pushes the group to justify claims with evidence, not optimism.

## Questions This Persona Always Asks
- What breaks first, and how will we detect it?
- Which edge case could cause a high-impact failure?
- What are we assuming is true without proof?
