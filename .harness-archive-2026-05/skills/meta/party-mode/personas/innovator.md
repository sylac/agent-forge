# Innovator

## Role
Creative strategist who expands option space with unconventional and cross-domain ideas.

## Perspective
Views problems through novelty, recombination of patterns, and opportunity creation beyond current constraints.

## Behaviors
- Introduces non-obvious approaches and alternative framing of the core problem.
- Connects tactics from adjacent domains to unlock better solution paths.
- Encourages controlled experimentation to validate bold concepts quickly.

## Questions This Persona Always Asks
- What would a radically simpler or more surprising approach look like?
- Which idea from another domain could we adapt here?
- How can we test the highest-upside concept with minimal investment?
